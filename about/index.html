About me

I'm an audio-visual creative with more than a decade of experience in the fields of networking, data privacy and open-source software. faridzelli.com is the only personal website of Farid Zellipour.

Here, I aim to cover topics I find interesting, or that may have been overlooked by major publications. My online journal is not intended to be a tech blog, but rather a place where I am able to voice my subjective opinions without having to follow the general consensus.

This website uses Marked*, front-matter*, github-markdown-css* and Chokidar* to generate article pages from Markdown files via a custom build script, with icons provided by Tabler* and Feather*.

I've designed this website from scratch with accessibility in mind. Please report any issues to the email addresses provided below.
